An affidavit is a written statement of fact voluntarily made by the person under an oath or affirmation before a person authorized by law. An affidavit to be valid has to be executed on Stamp paper or duly stamped otherwise and has to be notarised by a Notary public or commissioner of oath. This affidavit is voluntarily executed in order to attest to the truthfulness of the foregoing narration of facts and undertaking under pain of administrative, criminal and civil liabilities, and for whatever legal purpose it may serve.
